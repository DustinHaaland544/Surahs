Need to pray the dua e hajat after finishing the 2 rakat voluntury prayer of Salat Al Hajat

Dua E Hajat: Lā ilāha illallāhul-halīmul-karīm subhānallāhi rabil-a’rshil-a’dhīm al-hamdu lillahi rabil-ā’lamīn asaluka mūjibāti 
rahmatik wa a’zāima maghfiratik wal-ghanīmata min kulli birr was-salāmata min kulli ithm lā tada’ lī dhamban illā ghafartah wa lā
hamman illa farrajtah wa lā hājatan hiya laka ridhan illa qadhaytahā yā arhamar-rāhimīn.

Meaning: There is no God but Allah, the Forbearing, the Most Generous. Glory be to Allah, Lord of tremendous throne. All praise is 
to Allah, Lord of the worlds. I ask you (O Allah) everything that leads to your mercy, and your tremendous forgiveness, enrichment 
in all good, and freedom from all sin. Do not leave a sin of mine (O Allah), except that you forgive it, nor any concern except that 
you create for it an opening, nor any need in which there is your good pleasure except that you fulfil it,O The Most Merciful!
