BISMILLAH IL-LAZEE LA YA-DUR-RU 
MA’AS-MIHI SHAI’UN FIL-ARDI 
WA LA FIS-SAMA’I, 
WA HU-WAS-SAMI’UL-‘ALIM

In the Name of Allah, Who with His Name
nothing can cause harm in the earth nor in
the heavens, and He is the All-Hearing, the
All-Knowing.

3x Morning after Fajr
& 3x After Asr onwards.
